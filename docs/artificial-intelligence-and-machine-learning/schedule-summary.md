# AI-Powered Summarization in Dashboard Scheduling

We’ve enhanced Bold BI’s scheduling feature by introducing support for AI-generated insights.
When the AI Summary option is enabled during scheduling, the system automatically creates natural-language summaries based on the data from the selected widgets and dashboards. These insights are generated at the time of execution, ensuring they reflect the most current data.
The summaries are then included directly in the scheduled email, alongside the report, providing recipients with quick, meaningful context without needing to analyze the visuals in detail.

## Key Benefits

- Saves time with instant, automated summary generation.  
- Improves clarity through clear, actionable insights from data.  
- Ensures accuracy using real-time widget data at export time.

## How to Enable AI Summary

1. In the scheduling window, locate the **"Enable AI Summary"** toggle.
![Schedule summary](/static/assets/artificial-intelligence-and-machine-learning/images/schedule-summary/enablesummary.png)

2. Turn it **ON**.  
![Schedule summary](/static/assets/artificial-intelligence-and-machine-learning/images/schedule-summary/enabledsummary.png)

3. Complete your schedule settings and save. 

4. All scheduled reports will now include an **AI-generated summary**.
